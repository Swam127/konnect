KONNECT



Why KONNECT ?


Konnect brings all NGOs on a common platform & provides them the ability to get resources & volunteers that they need in a timely and effective manner. It also provides users data at a central point where they can donate their items, money or time without the hassles to look around & call. The users can now understand who is the recipient in need of these items. It establishes instant connection between the donors and the recipient. It motivates people to volunteer their services which is actually is the need of the hour. It is a great platform to connect job seekers with the right employer.

Konnect is one stop App for all donation & volunteering needs!!




Primary Objectives of KONNECT


1) Connecting people with common vision & common goal, which is to Help to Hope for a better tomorrow for all.

2) Encouraging adults and kids to volunteer time for social cause.

3) Encouraging Corporate Social Responsibility.

4) Conducting various drives in Corporates, Schools, Colleges, Societies etc.

5) Establish connection, join hands, bring everyone together & make a difference for all those in need.
